## Content Tips

1. **Use High-Quality Images** - Feature your best student work and campus photos
2. **Authentic Testimonials** - Share real student stories and quotes
3. **Consistent Branding** - Use the academy's gold color (#D4AF37) and professional tone
4. **Call-to-Actions** - Always direct to website or contact info
5. **Engagement** - Respond to comments and DMs promptly
6. **Hashtag Strategy** - Mix popular and niche hashtags
7. **Timing** - Post when your audience is most active (typically 9-11 AM, 6-8 PM)
8. **Variety** - Mix educational content, testimonials, behind-the-scenes, and promotional posts
